cocoa-spiced beef tenderloin with pineapple salsa

Latin American flavors alive in this festive beef dish with fruity salsa

ingredients

  • ½ Tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 beef tenderloin roast (16 oz)
  • for salsa
    • ½C canned diced pineapple, in fruit juice, chopped into small pieces
    • red onon, miced ( quarter cup)
    • frsh cilantro, rinsed,dried, and chopped (half a cup)
    • 1 tables spoon lemon juice

  • for seasoning

    • 1 tablespoon ground black pepper
    • 1 tablespoon ground coriander
    • i tablespoon ground cinnamon
    • quarter tablespoon ground allspice
    • 1 tablespoon cocoa powder(unsweetened)
    • 2 tablespoons chili powder
    • quarter tablesppon salt

procedure

Prep time: 20 minutes Cook time: 20 minutes

Preheat oven to 375 ºF.

Let sit for 10 to 15 minutes to marinate while preparing the seasoning and cooking the meat. For the salsa, combine all ingredients and toss well.

Lightly oil the tenderloin and spread an even layer of the dry seasoning over the entire roast. For the beef tenderloin seasoning, combine all

and roast for 10 to15 minutes (to a minimum internal temperature of 145 ºF). Let cool for 5 minutes before carving into 16 slices (1 ounce each). Place the seasoned roast on a roasting or broiling pan

Serve four slices of the tenderloin with ¼ cup salsa on the side.

Delicious with a side of rice and Grilled Romaine Lettuce With Caesar Dressing
